The temple

Dear Devotees,
Muthukur Sri Lakshmi Narayana Swami Temple is the historical temple. The temple have 2 idols, Sri Lakshmi Narayana Swami and Sri MahaLakshmi. It was constructed in 13th century. It is also said that this temple was built at the end of Dwaparayuga and the wellknown sulprit MAYA designed the main idol. So this idol have a history of 5100 years and the temple have history of 1300 years. This temple is located in the middle of the pond, during heavy rain the water surrounds the temple and it forms like an island. 10 years ago the renovation program was performed by our family. But before that our ancestors has performed renovations twice. Devotees collected the funds from family and friends to develop the temple. A group of devotees is formed and registerd. By the year 2002 all the renovation and devleopment work was completed. Form then kalyanothsava and other functions are performed every year.

Location

Muthukur, Gudibanda Mandal, Ananthapuram Dist, Andhra Pradesh
